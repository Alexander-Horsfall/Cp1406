Goals

The purpose of this website is to improve the number of people playing at Slammers each week by at least 50%. The site is needed as a form of advertisement to the younger generation, it will promote the sport and compeitions held.

Success Evaluation

The success of the website will be measured by the amount of people signing up to play in combination with the amount of people turning up to play.

Target Audience

The target audience for the site lie between the ages of 15 and 30. The site aims to appeal to kids in high school, this will be achieved through the use of images and site layout.
